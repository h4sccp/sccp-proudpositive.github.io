Empower Yourself with Knowledge

Understanding STIs and HIV

The Proud and Positive movement empowers you with knowledge to protect your health. STIs and HIV can affect anyone, but awareness is your first defense.

  • Transmission: STIs and HIV spread primarily through unprotected sexual contact or shared needles.
  • Symptoms: Many STIs are asymptomatic. Watch for signs like discharge, sores, or pain, and get tested regularly.
  • Early Detection: Timely testing prevents complications and reduces transmission. Our clinic offers confidential testing for all, including LGBTQ+ communities.

Prevention: Stay Safe, Stay Proud

Prevention is empowerment. Adopt these practices to stay healthy:

  • Safe Sex: Use condoms consistently to reduce STI/HIV risk.
  • Regular Testing: Routine testing is key, especially for high-risk groups. We provide inclusive, stigma-free testing.
  • PrEP: Ask about Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis for HIV prevention, tailored for diverse communities.
  • Partner Communication: Discuss sexual health openly with partners to foster trust.
  • Vaccinations: Explore vaccines like HPV to protect your health.

Breaking Stigma with Proud and Positive

Stigma shouldn’t stop you from seeking care. Our Proud and Positive campaign, including the 2024 Pride Parade and youth advocacy, creates a welcoming space for all.

  • Confidentiality: Your privacy is guaranteed in all services.

  • Community Advocacy: Join our events like Parol ng Pag-asa to fight stigma through education and celebration.
